    Project Idea

    In today's world, it is said that attention is the modern currency. With that being said, I have decided to take part by creating and designing a platform for content creators and viewers to enjoy. In order to stand out from similar platforms such as TikTok, Instagram and others alike, this app’s main feature is to provide users with a limited amount of likes per day. The likes/hearts given to content creator’s videos will act as an in-app currency that can be traded in for fiat or for other premium utilities only purchasable by likes. By implementing this twist, hopefully it can be unique enough for the masses to be entertained by.

    With around 1 billion active monthly users for TikTok and Instagram and approximately 2 billion active monthly users for Youtbe, According to, “smperth.com”, it's clear that there is an ever growing market for content-sharing platforms. Keeping this fact in mind, it would be interesting to see what content the majority of people value more as they swipe to find content worthy enough for their likes. To add on, as users seek to find content that is deserving for their “like”, it increases the possibility of a higher user watchtime which is beneficial for the app’s reputation. Finally, this feature creates a supply and demand reaction for likes which ultimately leads to only quality content to be produced and given recognition.

    Description of features.
    After downloading the app, the user will be greeted with a prompt to create an account or be a guest with limited access to the features. To create a verified account, the user will be required to verify their age, confirm their email address or phone number. By verifying an account, the user gains access to all the features of the app and can start off by completing their profile by adding their profile picture, creating a bio and choosing the option to be a private or public account. In the profile section of the app, the upper portion will display the user’s number of posts, followers, following and likes, whilst the bottom showcases their public content for people to watch. In the top right of the profile, there will be an icon of three lines that will pop up to show icons of the user’s account information, saved and liked videos, messages, settings and options.

    In order to move from one section of the app to another, there will be icons displayed through the very top and bottom portion of the layout. The top icons presented will represent followed content and a default content page. The bottom icons will represent the search tab, upload feature, activity tab and user profile. In the followed content tab, it will display content from creators that the user follows which can be watched by swiping left for a new video, and swiping right to go back to the previous video. Similarly, the default content page will have the same mechanics but will instead be a curated feed based on the user’s likings and engagement. The search tab will allow users to search for other accounts and will act as an explore page full of trending videos. The upload feature is a way for users to film/upload videos, edit them and apply filters or songs. Lastly, the activity tab displays a feed of new likes, comments and follows that the user gains. When watching videos, the creator’s profile will be shown on the screen along with a like, comment and share icons. There will also be an option to report another user’s account for violating any terms and conditions.

    Each day, the user will be given 10 likes to spend and will be worth 0.03 usd. These likes can then be cashed in or be spent on premium features that can make their account stand out. These premium features can be profile banners, gifs as their profile picture, coloured comments and much more. However, a content creator will be unable to cash in their likes until they reach 10,000 followers. As this app continues to grow, so will the improvements in order to ensure user satisfaction.

    Tools and Technologies
    As anyone would guess, it would be greatly difficult to develop and design an app single-handedly. Therefore, the first tool needed would be an experienced team of app developers and designers. By forming a team, it allows different parts of the apps to be built simultaneously, thus making it more efficient. The softwares needed are Flutter and Dart. Flutter is a free and open-source mobile UI framework created by Google (Gaël Thomas 2019, freecodecamp.org) that uses the programming language called Dart . Furthermore, flutter creates native machine code through your code and offers pre-built widgets which ultimately saves time creating code for both IOS and Android platforms. Lastly, the hardware needed would be a computer and the accessories in order to write and store the code.

    Skills Required
    The skills required to complete this project would be to first have a thorough understanding of javascript, swift or java programming languages. By having heavy experience in some or all of these languages, it makes learning the Dart language easier due to similarities. Another skill needed is being familiar with not only the Drat language but the flutter software and their features. Learning these skills are feasible but would take some years to master, but finding people with these skills is much faster to find but will be costly.

    If this app does become successful, it will be a leading competitor in the content platform market and create a space for up and coming content creators to shine. Furthermore, we will be able to monetize through paid sponsorships of videos and ads or even sell the platform. Overall, I believe this development will help make the market even more competitive and is a great way to build a reputation in the IT field.
